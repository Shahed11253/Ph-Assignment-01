Frequently Asked Questions

How Can I Get The Conference Videos?

❯

All registered participants will receive lifetime or 30-day access to the session recordings via their registered email dashboard within 24 hours after the event concludes.

How Much Time Will It Take To Finish The Tracks?

❯

The conference spans over three intense days, featuring parallel tracks for AI/ML, Frontend, and Cloud & DevOps. Each daily track runs for approximately 6 to 7 hours.

What I Will Learn From This DevConf?

❯

You will gain insights into next-generation frontend frameworks, large language models (LLMs), practical secure infrastructure building, and networking opportunities with tech leaders.

For whom is this conference?

❯

This event is designed for software developers, UI/UX engineers, system administrators, security analysts, student builders, and technology founders.

What educational background is required?

❯

There are no strict educational restrictions. A basic understanding of programming concepts, software development lifecycles, or modern web technologies is helpful.

How can I get access to videos offline?

❯

Pro and Team pass holders can download the video modules directly through the DevConf official app for seamless offline viewing anytime.
